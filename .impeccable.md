# Design Context

### Users
- Long-term live-stream viewers who use the app frequently and care about efficiency, stability, and readability.
- Desktop-first for this refactor: large-window viewing, multi-tasking (PiP/transparent floating window), keyboard and tray-based control.

### Brand Personality
- Restrained, premium, minimal.
- Calm and focused: the UI should never compete with the video/content.
- Trustworthy and precise: clear hierarchy, consistent spacing, predictable interactions.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Content-first layout with subtle structure (thin dividers, soft elevation, low-contrast surfaces).
- Light and dark themes only, both polished to parity.
- Avoid busy gradients, heavy shadows, and decorative effects.

### Design Principles
1. Content is the hero: prioritize video, chat, and key controls; everything else is secondary.
2. Dense but legible: support heavy users with information density while keeping typography and spacing clean.
3. Quiet affordances: subtle separators and states; no noisy chrome.
4. Desktop-native: excellent keyboard/mouse ergonomics, multi-window flows, tray/taskbar integration.
5. Theme parity: light/dark behave consistently with tuned contrast and surface layering.
